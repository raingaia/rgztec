Category overview

Sensors & Connectivity covers the hardware that sees, measures and transmits — and the UI you need to trust that layer.

These kits focus on visibility: how strong the link is, how noisy the environment is, which nodes are online, and which parts of the sensor stack are drifting or failing. It’s about making invisible signals and numbers intuitive on-screen.
• Dashboards that surface readings from environmental, energy, industrial and structural sensors.
• Connectivity panels that track RSSI, SNR, packet success, retries, latency and link quality over time.
• Mesh- and gateway-aware layouts that show how data flows from node to node, including hop paths and bottlenecks.

FAQ

Can we adapt these for our own RF stack? Yes. Metrics and labels are structured so you can rename them to match your protocols and hardware.
Do you support both indoor and outdoor sensors? Layouts are agnostic: you can use them for buildings, cities, plants, campuses or mixed deployments.
Can this be used for network operations? Yes. Many patterns fit directly into NOC/SOC-style consoles focused on RF and sensor layers.
