Category overview

Smart Controllers is where Hardware Lab covers the brain of hardware: boards and devices that make other things move, switch, heat or react.

These kits are designed for control UX: setting targets, applying overrides, managing schedules and exposing safe, understandable interfaces for technical and non-technical operators.
• Layouts for controller dashboards — from compact device-level views to multi-zone control surfaces.
• Patterns for modes (manual/auto), interlocks, safety states, and "are you sure?" confirmation paths.
• Config flows for controllers that sit between field devices and cloud logic: mapping inputs, outputs and rules.

FAQ

Can we adapt these for PLC or DDC systems? Yes. Many patterns are designed with PLC, DDC and other controller architectures in mind.
Do you cover both small and large systems? Layouts range from compact single-controller views to multi-zone and multi-device orchestration surfaces.
What about safety reviews? Several kits are structured to make safety reviews and audits easier with clear states and logs.
