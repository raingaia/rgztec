Category overview

AI Accelerators covers the UIs that sit directly on top of tensor compute: model runs, performance envelopes and device health.

These kits are made for teams who ship actual AI workloads on boards, boxes and edge racks — not just cloud-only prototypes. Latency, throughput, power, temperature and accuracy all surface here.
• Model-centric dashboards that show inference rates, queues, errors and input/output stats in real time.
• Accelerator panels for memory, compute lanes, thermal headroom, throttling and performance tuning.
• Pipeline layouts for full AI flows: data in, transforms, inference, post-processing and monitoring.

FAQ

Can we use this for robotics and CV? Yes. These layouts are ideal for cameras, robots and real-time perception stacks using on-device AI.
Does it assume a specific hardware vendor? No. Metrics and labels are neutral so you can adapt them to your own accelerators and runtimes.
Can this work with hybrid inference? Absolutely. Many components are designed for split workloads, where some calls run on devices and others in the cloud.
