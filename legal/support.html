Support & Updates Policy

Last updated: September 9, 2025

This Support & Updates Policy applies to Items sold on the RGZTEC marketplace operated by Raingaia LLC d/b/a RGZTEC. It explains what is covered by support, target response times, and how updates are delivered.

1) Coverage (What’s Included)

  • Installation guidance per the documentation (prerequisites, build steps, configuration).
  • Bug fixes for defects that prevent advertised features from working in supported environments.
  • Compatibility assistance with the frameworks/browsers/engines explicitly listed in the Item description.
  • Security patches for vulnerabilities discovered in the Item.

2) Exclusions (What’s Not Included)

  • Custom features, bespoke development, or major rewrites/migrations.
  • Integration work with third-party systems not listed as supported.
  • Performance tuning for unique environments or resource constraints.
  • Training, consulting, or on-premises installation services.

3) Support Windows

  • Regular License: support & updates for 6 months from purchase.
  • Extended / Agency: support & updates for 12 months from purchase.
  • Critical security patches may be provided beyond these windows at Seller’s discretion for the most recent minor version.

4) Severity & Target Response Times

SeverityDescriptionTarget Response
Critical (S1)Production-stopping issue with no workaround; security breach/vulnerability.Business day 1 (or sooner)
High (S2)Major functionality broken; workaround exists but impacts delivery.Within 2 business days
Normal (S3)Minor bug, cosmetic issue, clarification.Within 3 business days

Targets are goals, not guarantees. Holidays and peak release periods may affect times. Priority queues may apply for Extended/Agency licenses.

5) How to Request Support

  1. Open a ticket by emailing support@rgztec.com with your Order ID, Item name/version, environment details, and reproduction steps.
  2. Attach logs, screenshots, and any error traces. Provide temporary access (if needed and safe) to reproduce issues.
  3. We may request additional information or suggest upgrades/patches.

6) Updates & Delivery

  • Updates are delivered via download portal/repository access. You are responsible for applying updates.
  • Versioning follows semantic versioning where feasible (MAJOR.MINOR.PATCH).
  • Breaking changes are highlighted in release notes and may require migration work (outside support scope unless stated).

7) Security & Vulnerability Handling

  • Report suspected vulnerabilities privately to security@rgztec.com (or via support). Do not publicly disclose before a fix/workaround is available.
  • Sellers should target a fix or mitigation for high-severity vulnerabilities within 72 hours where feasible.

8) Deprecation & End of Life

  • When a dependency or platform version reaches end-of-life, continued compatibility may require a paid upgrade or new major version.
  • Deprecated features will be announced in release notes with reasonable notice.

9) Fair Use

We may limit or pause support in cases of abusive behavior, excessive repetitive tickets, or requests that fall outside scope. Support is provided in English during business hours unless otherwise stated.

10) Relationship to Other Policies

This policy is read together with the Terms of Service, Buyer Terms & EULA, and Seller Agreement.
