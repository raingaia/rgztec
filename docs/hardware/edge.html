Category overview

Edge Computing is where data is processed close to where it’s created: in gateways, boxes, racks and on-device accelerators.

These kits are designed for teams that run real workloads at the edge: streaming transforms, local inference, rule engines and time-critical decisions that cannot wait for a round trip to the cloud.
• Node and site-level views to see what’s running where, with clear health and capacity signals.
• Streaming dashboards that show windows, batches, rates and backpressure in a way operators can act on.
• Workflows that connect edge, sensors and cloud so product teams can explain their architecture visually.
