Category overview

IoT Devices is the part of Hardware Lab that focuses on interfaces for hubs, gateways, smart sensors and edge-connected assets.

These kits are built for teams who ship real connected hardware: pairing flows that survive real networks, dashboards that can handle dozens of metrics, and status surfaces that make sense to operations teams during incidents.
• End-to-end paths for onboarding devices: discovery, pairing, authentication, firmware and first data in.
• Fleet dashboards for gateways and sensors, built with clear health, connectivity and alert states.
• Patterns for offline, degraded and error conditions — not just happy-path marketing screens.
